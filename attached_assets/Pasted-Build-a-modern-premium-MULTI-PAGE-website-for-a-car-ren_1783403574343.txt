Shared navbar: logo, Home / Fleet / Services / Packages / Gallery / 
About / Contact, sticky on scroll, mobile hamburger menu with slide-in

copyright.

BRAND
- Business type: Private car rental + outstation tour packages
- Service cities: Pune, Mumbai, Nashik (also covers Shirdi, Goa, 
  Mahabaleshwar, Lonavala)
- Positioning: Verified drivers, transparent per-km pricing, 
  24/7 dispatch, no hidden fees
- Contact: Phone/WhatsApp number, email, GST invoices, corporate 
  billing available

DESIGN DIRECTION (make it feel premium, not templated)
- Distinctive palette (avoid generic blue-and-white travel look) — 
  e.g. deep charcoal/graphite + warm amber/copper accent, or rich 
  navy + gold
- Strong display serif or geometric sans for headings, clean sans 
  for body
- Generous whitespace, large hero imagery, subtle gradient/grain 
  overlays for depth
- Micro-interactions: scroll-triggered fade/slide-ins, hover lift + 
  shadow on cards, animated counters, smooth page-to-page transitions
- Mobile-first responsive layout throughout

PAGE-BY-PAGE CONTENT

Home (/)
- Hero: headline, subheadline, dual CTA (Reserve Now / WhatsApp), 
  rotating/parallax background images
- Trust stats bar: trips completed, average rating, dispatch hours, 
  verified drivers count
- Fleet preview (3-4 cards, link to /fleet)
- Popular routes grid (link to /packages or /contact for quotes)
- Services preview grid (link to /services)
- Testimonials carousel
- FAQ accordion
- CTA banner linking to /contact

Fleet (/fleet)
- Full vehicle lineup: photo, name, passenger/bag capacity, per-km 
  rate, "Book This Car" CTA per card
- Filter/sort by capacity or price (nice-to-have)

Services (/services)
- Detailed cards: airport transfers, one-way trips, round trips, 
  corporate travel, wedding car rental, outstation taxi, custom tours
- Each with icon, description, and inquiry CTA

Packages (/packages)
- Grid of tour packages (card: destination image, duration, 
  starting price, "View Details" → links to /packages/[slug])

Package detail (/packages/[package-name])
- Hero image, itinerary breakdown (day-by-day), inclusions/exclusions, 
  price, booking CTA

Gallery (/gallery)
- Masonry/grid of vehicle and destination images, lightbox on click

About (/about)
- Company story, verified-driver process, coverage area map, 
  founder/team blurb (optional), stats

Contact (/contact)
- Inquiry form (name, phone, route, date, vehicle type, message), 
  phone/WhatsApp/email, embedded map placeholder
